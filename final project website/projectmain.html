Airplane using no Gas

Making aircraft fuel from sunlight and air, November 4, 2021

Electric News

Scientists at ETH Zurich have built a plant that can produce carbon-neutral liquid fuels from sunlight and air. The next goal will be to take this technology to industrial scale and achieve competitiveness. In a paper published in the journal Nature, researchers from Zurich and Potsdam describe how this novel solar reactor functions and outline a policy framework that would provide incentives to expand the production of "solar kerosene."


Tesla Has Officially Become Big Tech After Surging Beyond $1 Trillion in Market Value.

Money Value ,Oct 25, 2021

Electric News


It seemed both impossible and inevitable, and it's finally happened. Elon Musk's Tesla has officially surpassed $1 trillion in market capitalization on Monday, becoming the fifth firm in the country to do so, after Microsoft, Alphabet, Apple, and Amazon, according to an initial report from Reuters. Astronomers may have discovered a planet outside of our galaxy

Oct 27, 2021

Space


Signs of a planet transiting a star outside of the Milky Way galaxy may have been detected for the first time. This intriguing result, using NASA's Chandra X-ray Observatory, opens up a new window to search for exoplanets at greater distances than ever before.

University of California Los Angeles, November 5, 2021

Environment
